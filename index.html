Explanations of the different game modes :

Human

This mode let you play freely.

Random

In this mode, the player is the computer. It plays randomly.

MiniMax Algorithm

In this mode, the player is an Artificial Intelligence. It works by making the tree of all possibilities.

Alpha-Beta Pruning

In this mode, the player is an Artificial Intelligence. It works by making the tree of all possibilities but it removes useless branches.

Most Wins Heuristic

In this mode, the player is an Artificial Intelligence. The AI compares the number of its chances of winning to that of its opponent.
